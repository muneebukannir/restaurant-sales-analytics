# 🍔 Restaurant Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyses a restaurant's sales performance using PostgreSQL and Tableau to uncover business insights and provide management with actionable recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The restaurant owner wanted to understand the business performance over the past two years in order to answer key business questions:

- Is the business growing?
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- When is the restaurant busiest?
- How do customers prefer to pay?
- Which areas require management attention?
